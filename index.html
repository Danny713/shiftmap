Elevator Pitch

So here’s the problem: you are a student employee here at Princeton University and then something comes up and you can no longer make your shift. Current solution: send a storm of unwanted emails littering everyone’s inboxes, requesting for subs. Our solution: interactive one-stop platform for employees to add, drop, swap and sub shifts that is intuitive and convenient. So we introduce ShiftMap.
